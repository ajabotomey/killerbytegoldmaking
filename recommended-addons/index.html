    Basic Goldmaking Addons

    In general, I tend to use TradeSkillMaster or TSM because it is arguably the best goldmaking toolkit out there for goldmakers. There are plenty of guides out there on how to use TSM from HazelNuttyGames to SamadanPlaysWoW or even right here. I tend to use TSM groups from both Sheyrah and Torack which imports profession groups. I may write some additional guides if time permits.

    Addons for gathering professions

    I have three main addons for gathering as well as creating routes based on the nodes that I find.

    1. GatherMate 2 GatherMate 2 marks the map with nodes from herbing and mining.

    2. Routes Routes takes the data from GatherMate 2 and converts that into a route that can me followed on a minimap or through FarmHUD.

    3. FarmHUD FarmHUD takes the GatherMate2 and Routes overlay from the minimap and overlays it across your screen
